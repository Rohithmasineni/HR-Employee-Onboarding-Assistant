EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING CHECKLIST — COMPLETE GUIDE
================================================
Company: TechCorp Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
Document Owner: HR Department
Last Updated: April 2025

This checklist applies to ALL new employees. However, certain steps are specific to
FRESHERS (no prior work experience) and EXPERIENCED HIRES (joining from another company).
Each step is clearly tagged: [ALL], [FRESHER ONLY], or [EXPERIENCED ONLY].

DAY 1 — JOINING DAY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

ADMINISTRATIVE SETUP [ALL]
---------------------------
[ ] Report to the Security/Admin desk and collect your Photo ID Card
[ ] Get your system (laptop/desktop) allocated by the IT team
[ ] Set up your official company email account (IT will provide credentials)
[ ] Sign and submit the Offer Letter Acceptance form to HR
[ ] Complete and submit the Emergency Contact Information form
[ ] Receive your employee ID card and access badge

DOCUMENT SUBMISSION — DAY 1 [ALL]
-----------------------------------
Submit the following to HR on Day 1. Documents vary based on your experience level.

Documents required from ALL employees (Fresher + Experienced):
  ✅ 10th and 12th Mark Sheets (originals for verification, photocopies retained)
  ✅ Graduation/Post-Graduation degree certificate or provisional certificate
  ✅ All semester mark sheets (UG/PG)
  ✅ PAN Card photocopy (mandatory for payroll setup)
  ✅ Aadhaar Card photocopy (for identity verification)
  ✅ Passport-size photographs (4 copies)
  ✅ Bank account details — cancelled cheque or passbook first page (for salary credit)
  ✅ Signed Employment Agreement / Bond (if applicable)
  ✅ Medical fitness certificate (if required by company policy)

Additional documents for EXPERIENCED HIRES only [EXPERIENCED ONLY]:
  ✅ Relieving Letter from your previous employer
       — This is MANDATORY for experienced hires to confirm clean exit from last company
       — Must be on company letterhead with HR/manager signature
       — If not available on Day 1, submit within 30 days (joining may be conditional)
  ✅ Experience Letter from previous employer(s)
  ✅ Last 3 months' salary slips from previous company
  ✅ Form 16 or Income Tax documents from previous employer (for tax calculation)
  ✅ Background verification consent form (BGV is conducted for all experienced hires)

NOTE FOR FRESHERS [FRESHER ONLY]:
  ℹ️ If you are joining as a fresher (no prior work experience), you do NOT need:
     - Relieving Letter (not applicable — you have no previous employer)
     - Experience Letter
     - Previous salary slips or Form 16
  ✅ Freshers only need: Mark sheets, degree certificate, PAN, Aadhaar, bank details,
     passport photos, and the signed employment agreement.
  ℹ️ If you have done internships, an internship completion certificate is optional but appreciated.

WEEK 1 — ORIENTATION WEEK
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

HR & CULTURE ORIENTATION [ALL]
--------------------------------
[ ] Attend the HR Orientation session (2–3 hours on Day 1 or Day 2)
      Topics covered:
      - Company history, mission, values, and culture
      - Organizational structure and departments
      - HR policies: leave, attendance, work timings, dress code
      - Benefits: health insurance, PF, gratuity, reimbursements
      - Code of conduct, POSH policy, and anti-harassment guidelines
      - Grievance redressal process

IT SETUP [ALL]
---------------
[ ] Set up VPN access (required for remote/hybrid work)
[ ] Install and configure internal tools:
      - Slack (team communication)
      - Jira (project and task tracking)
      - Confluence (documentation and knowledge base)
      - Microsoft 365 / Google Workspace (email, calendar, docs)
[ ] Set up multi-factor authentication (MFA) on your company account
[ ] Get access to relevant GitHub/GitLab repositories (coordinate with your team lead)
[ ] Configure your development environment if you are in a technical role

COMPLIANCE TRAINING [ALL]
---------------------------
[ ] Complete mandatory online compliance modules (available on HR portal):
      - Information Security & Data Privacy
      - POSH (Prevention of Sexual Harassment) awareness
      - Code of Conduct
      - Anti-Bribery and Corruption Policy
      (All modules must be completed within the first 2 weeks)

PAYROLL & BENEFITS ENROLLMENT [ALL]
-------------------------------------
[ ] Submit bank account details for salary processing (if not done on Day 1)
[ ] Enroll in company health insurance (self + dependents) — submit within 7 days of joining
[ ] Confirm PF (Provident Fund) nomination form submission
[ ] Review and confirm CTC breakup with HR (understand your salary structure)
[ ] Register on the attendance management system (biometric or app-based)

FRESHER-SPECIFIC ORIENTATION SESSIONS [FRESHER ONLY]
------------------------------------------------------
[ ] Attend the Fresher Welcome Program (if applicable) — separate from general HR orientation
[ ] Introduction to professional workplace etiquette session
[ ] Mentor assignment: You will be assigned a buddy/mentor from your team for the first 90 days

WEEK 2 — ROLE ONBOARDING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

MANAGER & TEAM INTRODUCTIONS [ALL]
------------------------------------
[ ] Schedule and attend a formal 1:1 meeting with your Reporting Manager
      Topics to discuss in this meeting:
      - Your role, responsibilities, and KPIs
      - Team structure and key stakeholders
      - Current projects you will be contributing to
      - Tools, processes, and ways of working
      - Your 30-60-90 day plan
[ ] Get introduced to your immediate team members
[ ] Meet cross-functional stakeholders if relevant to your role
[ ] Understand the team's sprint/project cycle and attend stand-ups

PROJECT ONBOARDING [ALL]
--------------------------
[ ] Review product/project documentation (on Confluence or shared drive)
[ ] Understand the codebase architecture (for technical roles)
[ ] Shadow a senior team member for at least 2–3 days before taking independent tasks
[ ] Complete your first assigned task (typically a small, well-defined starter task)

FOR EXPERIENCED HIRES ONLY [EXPERIENCED ONLY]:
[ ] Background Verification (BGV) process will begin in Week 2
      - HR will share a BGV form — fill it accurately
      - Previous employer details, education, address proof required
      - BGV is typically completed in 2–3 weeks
      - Any discrepancy found in BGV can lead to offer revocation

BY END OF MONTH 1 — 30-DAY MILESTONES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[ ] Complete probation review form (submitted to HR and your manager)
[ ] Set and document 30-60-90 day goals with your manager
[ ] Ensure all IT access requests are resolved (no pending access blockers)
[ ] Confirm all compliance training modules are completed on the HR portal
[ ] Ensure health insurance enrollment is confirmed (HR will send confirmation)
[ ] Verify your first payslip on the HR portal for correctness
[ ] Update your profile on the internal employee directory

PROBATION PERIOD INFORMATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

- Duration            : 6 months from Date of Joining (standard)
- Leave during probation: Maximum 5 days (CL + SL combined). EL not applicable.
- Performance review  : Formal review at end of Month 3 and Month 6
- Confirmation        : Upon successful completion, HR issues a confirmation letter
- Extension           : Probation may be extended by up to 3 months based on performance
- Termination         : During probation, notice period is 2 weeks (not 1 month)

FREQUENTLY ASKED ONBOARDING QUESTIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Q: I am a fresher. Do I need to submit a Relieving Letter?
A: NO. Relieving Letters are only required for experienced hires who are joining from
   a previous company. Freshers with no prior employment do not need to submit one.

Q: I did an internship. Do I need an internship certificate?
A: It is not mandatory, but you are encouraged to submit an internship completion
   certificate if you have one. It helps in building your employee profile.

Q: What if I don't have my original degree certificate yet (awaiting convocation)?
A: You can submit a Provisional Certificate issued by your university. The original
   degree certificate must be submitted within 6 months of joining.

Q: When will I receive my first salary?
A: Salary is credited on the last working day of the month. If you join mid-month,
   the first month's salary will be pro-rated based on your date of joining.

Q: How do I apply for health insurance for my family?
A: Download the insurance enrollment form from the HR portal under "Benefits".
   Submit it within 7 days of joining to ensure coverage from Month 1.

Q: What is the notice period for a new joiner?
A: During probation (first 6 months): 2 weeks notice period.
   After confirmation: 1 month notice period (as per your offer letter).

Q: My previous company's relieving letter is delayed. What do I do?
A: Inform HR immediately. You may be allowed to join with a conditional offer,
   but the relieving letter must be submitted within 30–60 days as specified by HR.
